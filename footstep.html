Harnessing Human Energy for Everyday Needs

Our project aims to generate power using stairs in public places, enabling sustainable energy solutions for everyday applications.

Project Overview

This project focuses on utilizing kinetic energy from foot traffic on stairs to generate electricity, which can be used to power everyday devices and reduce dependence on non-renewable sources.

The primary goal is to create a system that efficiently captures human energy and converts it into electrical power using piezoelectric technology.

Key Features

  • Sustainable Energy

    Using foot traffic to generate power is an eco-friendly solution to meet energy demands in public spaces.

  • Simple Installation

    The system can be easily integrated into existing public infrastructure like staircases in high-traffic areas.

  • Efficient Power Generation

    Efficient conversion of kinetic energy into usable electricity to power small devices and lighting.
