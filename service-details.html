3. Methodology

3.1. Unique Features of Holes

  • No power dependence: Operates on battery and solar. Works without direct power connection.
  • Budget installation: Approx. 10,000 BDT, modular setup based on area size.
  • Fully automatic operation: Detects threats and activates response within 30 seconds.
  • Emergency message: Sends SMS with GPS location using GSM network.
  • Extensive mitigation: Activates alarm, shuts gas, powers sprinklers, and emergency lighting.

3.2. System Workflow (Updated with Power Depiction)

  1. Track: Smoke and gas sensors send data to the control unit.
  2. Response: Activates alarms, lights, gas shutoff, and sprinklers.
  3. Emergency alert: Sends SMS to user and nearest fire station with GPS.
  4. Guidance: Illuminates escape routes and triggers alarms for safety.

3.3. Interval Application

  • Residential: Works during power failure, protects from kitchen fires.
  • Restaurants: Prevents gas fires, low-cost and easy to install.
  • Industries: Monitors large areas, scalable design.
  • Offices: Safe for employees, reliable in unstable electricity zones.

3.4. Testing and Verification

  • Battery/solar powered for 72+ hours.
  • Main unit ~10,000 BDT. Users found setup satisfactory.
  • Responds in under 30 seconds.
  • SMS and GPS alerts work without internet.
